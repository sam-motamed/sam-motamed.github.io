Research

I'm broadly interested in unsuervised learning and generative vision models. My research aims to gain a better understandionhg of how to introduce user-intuitive control over generative models for content creation. I a also interested in bias mitigation in vision models, and harnessing the power of large vision and language models and adapting them to more personalized tasks using limited data.
